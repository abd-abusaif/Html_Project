About

CZN Burak, owned by Turkish chef Burak Özdemir, is a popular restaurant chain known for its unique presentation of Turkish cuisine and its viral social media presence. The restaurant offers a variety of traditional Turkish dishes with a modern twist, often presented in large portions and with theatrical flair. Burak Özdemir, also known as CZN Burak, is famous for his smiling face and engaging cooking videos, which have garnered millions of followers online.

The restaurant chain has locations in various cities, including Istanbul, Dubai, and Doha, attracting both locals and tourists. The menu typically includes kebabs, mezes, and various Turkish desserts, all prepared with high-quality ingredients and presented in an eye-catching manner. The dining experience at CZN Burak is as much about the visual appeal and showmanship as it is about the delicious food
